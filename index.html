Randomizer

To use: Every time you load/reload the page, a new random email is picked. The winning email is revealed when you click on the button, or you can open your developer tools console to reveal that same winner. To pick again, simply reload the page and press the button again.

This app is to help the American Gastroenterological Association (AGA) select a random winner for their giveaway prizes. If you would like to use this code to create your own app please click here to download the starter TypeScript w/Webpack app.
