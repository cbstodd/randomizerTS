Randomizer

To use: Every time you load/reload the page a new random email is picked. You can cross check the pick by opening up your developer tools console. The name from "Pick email" will match the one in the console. To pick again, simply press the "New pick" button or reload the page.

This app is to help the American Gastroenterological Association (AGA) select a random winner for their giveaway prizes. Click here to download the code.
